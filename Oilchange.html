OIL CHANGES

Motor oil helps keep your car's engine running smoothly while reducing the wear on all its moving parts. Over time, oil begins to breakdown and wear out, making it less effective at lubricating your engine and transferring heat. When it's time for an oil change, we have all your needs covered. Every oil change performed by our certified technicians includes up to five quarts of oil, a new oil filter, and a courtesy vehicle inspection. We use premium oil brands such as Quaker State, Pennzoil, and Mobil 1, and can meet any of your car's needs from conventional to high-mileage, synthetic blend, and fully synthetic oil changes. Check out our oil change options below.

Oil Change Services


Quaker State Conventional Package

The Quaker State Conventional package uses the appropriate Quaker State conventional motor oil for your vehicle and contributes to extending the life of your vehicle’s engine. This service also includes a free tire rotation and tread depth and tire pressure check to help extend the life of your tires.

Includes:

  • Up to five quarts of Quaker State Conventional Motor Oil

  • Oil filter replacement

  • Free Tire Rotation

  • Tread Depth and Tire Pressure Check


Pennzoil High Mileage or Semi-Synthetic Package

Recommended if your vehicle has 75,000 miles or more, high mileage motor oil is formulated with anti-wear agents and friction reducing additives to help protect an older engine and can protect against oil leaks. If you’ve noticed motor oil leaks on your garage floor, this is the oil for you.

Includes:

  • Up to five quarts of Pennzoil High Mileage or Semi-Synthetic Motor Oil**

  • Oil Filter replacement

  • Free tire rotation

  • Tread depth and tire pressure check


Mobil 1 Synthetic Package

Mobil 1 motor oil protects your engine in extreme temperatures and cleans virtually all engine sludge after just one oil change. Mobil 1 delivers outstanding performance and supports quick cold-weather starting. It’s also backed by Mobil 1’s own engine guarantee.

Includes:

  • Up to five quarts of Mobil 1 Synthetic Motor Oil

  • Oil filter replacement

  • Free Tire Rotation

  • Tread depth and tire pressure check

  • Courtesy Vehicle Inspection
