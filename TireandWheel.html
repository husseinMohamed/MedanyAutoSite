Tire and Wheel Service Packages


Tire Rotation Service

Scheduling a tire rotation is a quick and inexpensive way to maximize the life of your tires. Rotating your tires leads to a prolonged tread life and promotes even tread wear for all tires on your vehicle, which will lead to improved gas mileage, increased tire performance and an overall smoother, more comfortable ride.

Tire Rotation Service includes:

  • Rotation of tires on your vehicle to optimize tread life in accordance with the vehicle manufacturer’s recommendation

  • Tightening of lug nuts to manufacturer’s torque specification

  • Check and set tire pressure to your vehicle’s specification

  • Price applies to all four tires


Flat Tire Repair Service

Having a flat tire can be scary and frustrating, leaving you with a feeling of despair, as you are stranded on the side of the road. You are not alone. We are here - just a phone call away. Call us and we will arrange for a tow. Repairing or changing a flat tire can be dangerous and should be handled by specially trained personnel. We have the professionals, as well as the products and equipment to dismount, mount, balance and repair the tire correctly and guarantee the repair for the life of the tire.

Flat Tire Repair Service includes:

  • Complete internal & external inspection of damaged tire(s) to determine if repairable

  • Safe, reliable repair from inside tire

  • Rebalancing of wheel

  • Courtesy Vehicle Inspection


Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Check-up Service

Properly inflated tires are critical to stability, traction, steering and braking. A tire that is low on air decreases gas mileage. Improperly inflated tires also experience a shortened life due to uneven wear. Let the tire professionals at Pep Boys ensure your tire pressure monitoring system (TPMS) is functioning properly.

Tire Pressure Monitoring System Check-up Service includes:

  • Testing of each of your vehicle’s TPMS sensor batteries

  • Check and set tire pressure to your vehicle’s specification
